ZOZDLE

Daily · 5 letters

How to play

Guess the hidden word in 6 tries. The twist: each day the word is 4, 5 or 6 letters — you won't know which until you start.

Each guess must be a real word of the right length. After every guess the tiles light up:

Z green — right letter, right spot.

O amber — in the word, wrong spot.

X grey — not in the word.

Daily is the same puzzle for everyone — share your grid. Practice gives endless random words.
